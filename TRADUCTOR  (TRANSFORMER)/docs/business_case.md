# Business Case: Neural Machine Translation Implementation

## 📊 Executive Business Summary

### Problem Statement
Global enterprises face significant challenges in content localization, with traditional translation services creating bottlenecks in international expansion. Manual translation processes are:
- **Expensive**: $0.15-0.30 per word for professional translation
- **Slow**: 2-5 days turnaround for standard documents
- **Inconsistent**: Quality varies between translators
- **Unscalable**: Cannot handle high-volume content demands

### Solution Overview
Implementation of a custom Transformer-based neural machine translation system that provides:
- **Automated translation** with 95%+ accuracy for business content
- **Real-time processing** capability (50+ tokens/second)
- **Consistent quality** across all translated content
- **Cost-effective scaling** for enterprise volume requirements
